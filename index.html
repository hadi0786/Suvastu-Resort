RESTAURANT

Suvastu Restaurant provides a full service California-Mediterranean inspired restaurant and bar. Our menu - which uses the finest local and imported ingredients - was developed by our executive chefs and includes wood fired oven roasted meats and seafood, homemade pizzas and pastas, freshly baked breads, tapas and a wide array of salads and appetizers.

ROOM SERVICES

Room services are designed to provide ultimate convenience and comfort during your stay. Enjoy a seamless blend of luxury and personalized attention while indulging in our thoughtfully curated amenities. Let us cater to your every need, making your experience unforgettable.

Our room services include the option to dine in the privacy of your room, terrace, or garden. Additionally, you can enjoy meals and refreshments in our climate-controlled dining areas or outdoor seating with breathtaking ocean views.
